Hamburgisches Welt-Wirtschafts-Archiv (HWWA)

Folders by Commodity/ware Category System

In total 965 categories, 2891 folders (until 1949) and 841454 images on digitized microfilm.

The Commodities/Wares archive was organized primarily by type of ware, seconadarily by country (or “World” for general aspects). Larger folders were subdivided by a category sytem (pdf, in German), which is not adressable in the electronic folder.

Of the PM20 Commodities/Wares archives up to 1949, only a rough estimate of one-eighth of the digitized holdings are indexed in folders and accessible here. Many small and large commodities/wares such as grain, coffee, motor vehicle or metal are missing among the folders and are acceible only as sections of digitized microfilm under certain restrictions.

Further unprocessed material from the second filming (until 1960) is, for copyright reasons, accessible only in the ZBW reading room. The complete ware category system is available online.
