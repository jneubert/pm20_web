Access to PM20 folders via GND

Most, but not all folders for persons and organizations are attributed with identifiers from the Integrated Authority File.

Redirects from GND IDs

With the URL pattern

https://pm20.zbw.eu/gnd/{gnd-id}

the folder pages for persons and companies can be accessed. For persons, {gnd-id} is the GND ID without the hyphen before the check digit, for companies the hyphen is required (e.g., https://pm20.zbw.eu/gnd/213147-X).

BEACON files

Mechanism for inking and access via GND (see BEACON).

  • Persons (persistent URL: http://purl.org/pressemappe20/beaconlist/pe)
  • Institutions (persistent URL: http://purl.org/pressemappe20/beaconlist/co)
  • Subject headings (persistent URL: http://purl.org/pressemappe20/beaconlist/sh)
  • Wares (persistent URL: http://purl.org/pressemappe20/beaconlist/wa)
